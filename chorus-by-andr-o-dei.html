CHORUS 759 (001)
FROM THE SERIES CHORUSES ON ROUND CANVASES
ANDR-O-DEI
SEPTEMBER 2025
Title: Chorus 759 (001), from the series
Choruses on Round Canvases
Artist: Andr-o-Dei (Andrey Dei)
Date: September 2025
Medium: Canvas and permanent marker
Dimensions: Circular format
Description: The first work in the series,
featuring a dense composition of 759
white faces with black eyes and mouths
on a black ground. The faces vary in
size and together form a rhythmic
visual chorus.
Signature: Signed on the canvas edge
ANDR O DEI 2025

Дата: Сентябрь 2025
Материал: Холст и перманентный маркер
Формат: Круглый
